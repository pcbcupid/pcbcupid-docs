# Power Management

The Glyph-C6 supports multiple power sources and includes an integrated battery charger for Li-Po batteries.

## Power Sources

### USB Power (VBUS)

- **Voltage:** 5V DC from USB-C connector
- **Current:** Up to 500 mA (USB 2.0 standard)
- **Usage:** Powers the board and charges the battery simultaneously.

### Battery Power (+BATT)

- **Battery Type:** Single-cell Li-Po or Li-Ion (3.7V nominal)
- **Charging IC:** MCP73831-2-OT
- **Charge Voltage:** 4.20V (fixed)
- **Charge Current:** Set by external resistor on PROG pin (default ~500 mA)
- **Protection:** The charger includes thermal regulation and safety timers.

## Power Supplies on Board

| Supply | Voltage | Source          | Notes                          |
|--------|---------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| VBUS   | 5V      | USB-C           | Direct from USB connector      |
| +3.3V  | 3.3V    | On-board regulator | Powers ESP32-C6 and GPIOs    |
| +BATT  | 3.7V    | Battery         | Direct battery connection      |

## Measuring Battery Voltage

The **BATT_MSR** pin (pin 23) provides a scaled voltage proportional to the battery voltage. This pin can be connected to an ADC input on the ESP32-C6 for battery monitoring.

- **Scaling:** The voltage on BATT_MSR is typically 1/2 of the battery voltage (check schematic for exact divider).
- **ADC Range:** ESP32-C6 ADC range is 0–3.3V. Ensure the divided voltage stays within this range.

## Alternative Power Options

- **External 3.3V:** The +3.3V pins can be used to supply the board directly (e.g., from a regulated 3.3V source). Do not exceed 3.6V.
- **External 5V:** The VBUS pin can accept 5V from an external source (e.g., a power bank) even without USB data connection.

## Warnings

- **Reverse Polarity:** Do not reverse the battery connection. The +BATT pin is positive; GND is negative.
- **Overvoltage:** Do not apply more than 5.5V to VBUS or more than 4.5V to +BATT.
- **Short Circuits:** Avoid shorting +3.3V to GND. This can damage the voltage regulator.
- **Battery Charging:** Only charge single-cell Li-Po/Li-Ion batteries with the built-in charger. Do not charge other battery chemistries.
- **Heat:** The MCP73831 may get warm during charging. Ensure adequate ventilation.
