# Assembly Guide

## Header Installation

The Glyph-C6 board is designed for use with standard 2.54 mm pitch pin headers. Two rows of 14 pins are required.

### Recommended Headers

- **Top Row (Pins 1–16):** 1x16 male pin header (straight or right-angle)
- **Bottom Row (Pins 17–28):** 1x14 male pin header (straight or right-angle)

### Soldering Steps

1. **Prepare the Board:** Ensure the board is clean and free of debris.
2. **Insert Headers:** Place the headers into the through-holes from the top side of the board. For breadboard use, insert the headers with the long pins facing down.
3. **Secure Headers:** Use masking tape or a breadboard to hold the headers perpendicular to the board during soldering.
4. **Solder Pins:** Solder each pin on the bottom side of the board. Apply heat to the pad and pin, then add solder. Avoid bridging adjacent pins.
5. **Inspect:** Check for cold joints or bridges. Use a multimeter to verify continuity.

## Alternative Connection Methods

- **Wire Soldering:** For permanent installations, wires can be soldered directly to the through-holes.
- **Socket Headers:** Use female socket headers on a carrier board for easy swapping.

## Handling Precautions

- **ESD Protection:** Handle the board in an anti-static environment.
- **Heat:** Use a soldering iron temperature of 300–350°C. Avoid prolonged heating of pads.
- **Cleaning:** Remove flux residue with isopropyl alcohol after soldering.
