# T002 TerraLink A7672S EK - Assembly Guide

## Header Installation Options

The board comes with a 7-pin through-hole header (pins 1-7) for external connections. Choose the appropriate option:

### Option 1: Soldered Header (Recommended for Prototyping)
- **Header Type**: 2.54 mm pitch male pin header (straight or right-angle).
- **Installation**:
  1. Insert header from top side (component side) through the board.
  2. Solder pins on the bottom side.
  3. Ensure header is perpendicular to board.

### Option 2: Socket Header (For Modular Use)
- **Header Type**: 2.54 mm pitch female socket.
- **Installation**:
  1. Solder socket on top side.
  2. Use male header on your carrier board.

### Option 3: Direct Wire Soldering
- For permanent connections, solder wires directly to the through-hole pads.
- Use 22-26 AWG wire.
- Tin the pads first, then solder wires.

## Soldering Guide

### Tools Required
- Soldering iron (350°C – 380°C)
- Leaded or lead-free solder (0.8 mm – 1.0 mm diameter)
- Flux pen (optional)
- Desoldering wick (for corrections)
- Helping hands or PCB holder

### Steps
1. **Clean the board**: Wipe with isopropyl alcohol to remove any residue.
2. **Tin the pads**: Apply a small amount of solder to each pad.
3. **Position the header**: Insert header pins into the holes.
4. **Solder one pin**: Heat the pin and pad simultaneously, apply solder, then remove iron.
5. **Check alignment**: Ensure header is straight; reheat if needed.
6. **Solder remaining pins**: Work from one end to the other.
7. **Inspect**: Look for cold joints, bridges, or insufficient solder.

### Tips
- Use a fine tip iron for precision.
- Apply flux to pads for better wetting.
- Do not overheat pads (max 3 seconds per joint).
- For mounting holes, no soldering required (they are non-plated through holes).

## Mechanical Assembly

### Mounting to Enclosure
- Use M2.5 screws and standoffs (6 mm height recommended).
- Mounting hole diameter: 3.54 mm (accepts M2.5 or #4 screws).
- Torque: 0.2 Nm (do not overtighten).

### Antenna Connection
- The A7672S module requires an external LTE antenna.
- Use a U.FL to SMA pigtail or direct U.FL connection.
- Keep antenna away from noisy components.

### SIM Card
- Insert SIM card into the on-board holder (if present).
- Ensure correct orientation (notch alignment).
